# The Graduate's Guide — Product context

## Register

brand

## Purpose

Privacy-first desktop cockpit (Tauri) for a local job-search vault: Today missions, Queue, script reader with Copy, follow-ups, apply assist. User sends/submits outside the app.

## Users

Job seekers who want structured scripts and daily focus without cloud accounts or auto-apply.

## Brand personality

Direct, honest, technical, calm. No hype. Phase 1 scope is explicit.

## Anti-references

Fake in-app Send buttons, real employer names in mocks, SaaS hero metrics, cream landing pages, auto-apply bots.

## Strategic principles

1. Copy in app; Send on portal or mail client.
2. Local vault ownership.
3. Marketing matches shipped features.
