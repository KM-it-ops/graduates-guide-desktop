Phase 1 features

Shipped in Phase 1. Stub and out-of-scope rows are listed in the scope table below.

Today

One priority. Five missions max. Open script, check off, move on.

Queue

Ranked applications from your vault — see what matters next.

Script reader

Paste-ready blocks with Copy — letters, outreach, prep.

Follow-ups

Track who needs a nudge without leaving your local files.

Apply assist

Script reader in-app plus portal opener — copy, paste, submit yourself in the browser.

Vault import

Point at your folder on disk. Your vault stays on your machine — zip, encrypt, or sync it however you like outside the app.

Your data stays local by design.

No account. No hosted CV. Your folder, your machine, your sends.

  • Vault on disk — you pick the folder
  • No account required
  • Telemetry — none in Phase 1
  • API keys — OS keychain only
  • Crash reports — preference saved locally; no reporter shipped yet
  • Hosted CV — never; vault stays on your machine
  • CI / fixtures — synthetic only, never real vault data
